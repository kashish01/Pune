Adlabs Imagica



An international standard theme park near Mumbai, Pune and Lonavala, Imagica Theme Park offers entertainment, fun, action,relaxation, dining and shopping at a single location. With 25 rides and attractions, and several F&B outlets to choose from, Imagica Theme Park is the perfect destination for friends and family a like.

The theme park is located near the city of Khopoli on Mumbai-Pune expressway. The theme park has supposedly cost Rs 1,600-crore in the making and is on the lines of International theme parks. The theme park officially opened in April 2013. The theme park is spread over 300 acres of land and can accommodate as many as 20,000 visitors.

The entertainment zones are divided according to continent themes and includes the following Viva Europa, Arabia, Asiana, Americana, India and Jambo Africa. Each zones has rides that match the particular theme. The attractions at the theme park are Rajasaurus River Adventure, Salimgarh, Alibaba Aur Chalis Chorr, Mr. India- The Ride, I For India,Wrath of the Gods, Cinema 360- Prince of the Dark Waters, Deep Space, Gold Rush Express, Splash Ahoy!, Bandits of Robin Hood, Loch Ness Expplorers, Save the Pirate, Tubbby Takes Off, Mambo Chai Chama- Crazy Tea Cups, The Magic Carousel, Bump It Boats, D2 Dare Drop, Scream Machine and Nitro which will launch very soon.
